Angel

This is one of my favourite projects. It was birthed from playing around with someone else's sketch (I unfortunatley can't find the original to give credit). I think this the first time I used modulous, cos and sine in any programming capacity. I had many variations of the sketch but settled on the current version. I intially described it as a pheonix in a constant cycle of rising from the ashes to die and resurect again. Now that I look at agin it could also be a fallen angel finding redemption.

This sketch and many others I have made make me appreciate the verstility of programming. It's not about always "hacking" and Facebook, Google and other imagerys asscociated with programming. It can aslo be art and music and other field where programming seems the least likely to occur.
